# User Data Safety

At BooleanBites Ltd, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the safety of your data. This page outlines the data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

**Data Collected**

We collect the following types of data to provide and improve our Imagitor app:

* **Email Address:** Used for account creation, login, and essential account-related communications.
* **Display Name:** Used to personalize your experience within the app and for profile display.
* **Profile Photo:** Used to personalize your profile within the app.
* **Log Data:** Includes device information (IP address, device name, OS version), app usage data (time, date, configuration), and error logs. This data helps us diagnose and fix issues.
* **Analytics Data:** We use Google Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymized data about app usage to understand user behavior and improve our services.
* **Crash Data:** We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports to identify and fix bugs.
* **Purchase Data:** We use RevenueCat to manage and process in-app purchases.

**Use of Data**

We use your data for the following purposes:

* **Account Management:** To create, manage, and authenticate your user account.
* **Service Improvement:** To analyze app usage, identify errors, and improve our services.
* **Communication:** To communicate with you about your account and provide essential updates.
* **In-App Purchases:** To process and manage in-app purchases.
* **Troubleshooting & Support:** To diagnose and resolve technical issues.

**Data Security**

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement reasonable and appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

**Data Sharing**

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in our Privacy Policy. This includes:

* **Service Providers:** We may share data with third-party service providers who assist us in providing and improving our services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, RevenueCat). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

**User Control**

* **Account Deletion:** You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by navigating to your profile within the app and selecting "Delete" from the top menu.
* **Privacy Policy:** For more detailed information about our data practices, please refer to our full Privacy Policy.

**Children’s Privacy**

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

**Changes to This Page**

We may update this User Data Safety page from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new page on this site.

**Contact Us**

If you have any questions or concerns about our data safety practices, please contact us at `imagitor.app \[at\] gmail \[dot\] com`
